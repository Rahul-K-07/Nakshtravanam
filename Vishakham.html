Vishakham

Common name:

Vayangatha

Plant name:

Coffee Plum Tree

Scientific name:

Flacourtia jangomas

Ayurvedic properties:

Antioxidant properties and supports digestive health.

Description:

The Coffee Plum Tree, or Buchanania lanzan, produces small, sweet fruits that are enjoyed fresh or used in jams and jellies. Found in tropical regions, this tree thrives in various soil types and is often cultivated for its fruit. The tree is also valued for its wood, which is durable and used in local crafts. Additionally, the Coffee Plum Tree plays a role in local ecosystems, providing food and habitat for various wildlife. Its fruits are rich in nutrients, making it a valuable addition to local diets.
