Common name:

Vanchimaram

Plant name:

Indian Willow Tree

Scientific name:

Salix tetrasperma

Ayurvedic properties:

Anti-inflammatory and supports joint health..

Description:

The Indian Willow Tree, or Salix alba, is a graceful tree often found near rivers and wetlands. Known for its slender branches and slender leaves, it provides shade and erosion control. The tree is valued for its flexible wood, used in traditional crafts and furniture. Its leaves have medicinal properties and are sometimes used in herbal remedies. The Indian Willow supports local biodiversity, providing habitat for various birds and insects. Its adaptability to wet environments makes it an essential species for maintaining healthy ecosystems.
