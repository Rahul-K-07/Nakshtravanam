Ayurvedic properties:

Traditionally used for respiratory support and has anti-inflammatory properties.

Description:

Milkweed is essential for monarch butterflies, serving as the primary host plant for their larvae. The plant produces a milky latex that has medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies. Milkweed thrives in various habitats, including fields and roadsides, and supports a diverse range of pollinators. Its flowers attract bees and butterflies, promoting biodiversity. While some species can be invasive, their ecological importance cannot be overlooked.
