Plant name:

Vetty Fruit Tree

Scientific name:

Aporosa lindleyana

Ayurvedic properties:

Supports digestive health and has cooling properties.

Description:

The Vetty Fruit Tree is known for its small, edible fruits, which are enjoyed in local cuisines. This tree thrives in tropical regions and has various traditional medicinal uses, with parts of the tree used to treat ailments. Its attractive foliage and fruiting habit make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes. The Vetty Fruit Tree contributes to local biodiversity, providing food and habitat for various species. Its cultural significance and practical uses make it a valuable resource for communities that rely on traditional knowledge.
