Common name:

Karimbana

Plant name:

Palmyra Palm Tree

Scientific name:

Borassus flabellifer

Ayurvedic properties:

Supports digestive health, cooling properties, and is nutrient-rich.

Description:

The Palmyra Palm Tree, or Borassus flabellifer, is a tall palm known for its multiple uses. It produces sweet sap that can be harvested for beverages, and its fruits are edible. The leaves are used for thatching and weaving, while the trunk provides durable timber. This tree thrives in arid and tropical regions, making it essential for local communities. Palmyra Palms are often found in traditional agricultural systems and contribute to food security. Their ecological role includes providing habitat for various species, enhancing biodiversity in their environments.
