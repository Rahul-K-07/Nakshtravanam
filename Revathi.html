Revathi

Common name:

Ilippa

Plant name:

Indian butter tree

Scientific name:

Madhuca indica

Ayurvedic properties:

Known for its nourishing properties; used in traditional medicine for skin health.

Description:

The Indian Butter Tree, or Dillenia indica, produces large, edible fruits with a tangy flavor, often used in local cuisines. The tree thrives in tropical regions, providing shade and habitat for wildlife. Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine, showcasing its cultural significance. The Indian Butter Tree is appreciated for its attractive flowers and large, spreading canopy, making it a popular choice for gardens and parks. Its ecological contributions and the nutritional value of its fruits make it an important resource in many communities.
