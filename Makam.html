Common name:

Peraal

Plant name:

Banyan Tree

Scientific name:

Ficus benghalensis

Ayurvedic properties:

Supports respiratory health, cooling, and promotes emotional stability..

Description:

The Banyan Tree is a remarkable species known for its sprawling branches and aerial roots, which develop into new trunks. It symbolizes immortality and is often associated with spiritual significance in many cultures. This iconic tree provides extensive shade and serves as a gathering place for communities. Its large canopy supports various wildlife, including birds and insects, contributing to local ecosystems. The Banyan Tree's resilience allows it to thrive in diverse climates, making it an essential part of cultural landscapes in India and beyond.
