Utram

Common name:

Ithi

Plant name:

Golden Rumph’s Fig

Scientific name:

Ficus microcarpa

Ayurvedic properties:

Supports digestive health and has anti-inflammatory properties.

Description:

This fig tree, known for its small, edible fruits, is important in local diets and ecosystems. The fruits are sweet and attract birds and insects, aiding in pollination. Golden Rumph’s Fig is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in various soil types. The tree is also valued for its shade and aesthetic appeal, making it popular in landscaping. Its leaves have traditional medicinal uses, and the tree plays a role in promoting biodiversity, providing food and habitat for numerous species in its environment.
