Common name:

Njaval

Plant name:

Black plum Tree

Scientific name:

Syzygium cumini

Ayurvedic properties:

Antioxidant-rich, supports digestive health, helps regulate blood sugar, and is beneficial for heart health

Description:

The Black Plum Tree produces small, dark purple fruits known as jamun, which are sweet and tangy. Rich in antioxidants, the fruit is beneficial for managing diabetes and digestive health. The tree thrives in tropical climates and is also valued for its shade. Additionally, its leaves and bark are used in traditional medicine. This tree plays a significant role in local ecosystems, providing habitat for various wildlife. With its attractive foliage and fruit, it is often cultivated in gardens and urban areas for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
